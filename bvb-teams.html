My Favorite Teams of Borussia Dortmund

The First Team


Although I support Borussia Dortmund as a club and all its teams entirely, I do have my favorite teams amongst all of the different teams that the club has. The first one and probably most obvious one is that I am a huge fan of the first team of Mens Soccer for Borussia Dortmund. I really like the philosophy of attacking football but also the individual players on the team. I regularly watch games of the first team playing in the Bundesliga and I keep track of records and standings in the league. Now that Dortmund lost star striker Erling Haaland, who has left to play in the Premier League, I thought that there was going to be decreasing performances of the team this year. However, the team has been performing very good recently and each player is working harder to make the dream of the championship happen. Dortmund has a chance of becoming champions of the Bundesliga this year after over 10 years of dominance by Bayern Munich over the league.


The U23

However, I am also a big fan and supporter of the second team of Borussia Dortmund, also known as the U23 (which stands for Under 23). Within this team, young talents are given the opportunity of training hard, becoming better footballers, and then finally be an option for the first team of the club and becoming a professional footballer. Being in the U23 is considered to be semi-professional and therefore is a great place for talented but not advanced footballers. In the past, there have been many players coming from the U23 that have been offered a position within the first team due to good performances and good development within the second team. I really think that the second team of Dortmund is the key for maintaining a very good, young, and future-directed team.


A couple examples of players who have made it to the first team after being in the second team of the club are goalkeeper Marcel Lotka, goalkeeper Luca Unbehaun, central defender Antonios Papadopoulos, central defender Somaila Coulibaly, midfielder Abdoulaye Kamara, and midfielder Goekhan Guerpuez.


The E-Sports Team

E-Sports is competitive video gaming done by professional electronic gamers, or also known as E-gamers. E-sports and E-football have been increasingly trending over the past couple of years with huge tournaments being played across the globe with players from all around the world. The demand for E-sports has been as high as it has never been and professional football clubs are starting to pay attention to that and have mostly started to implement an E-Sports or E-football in the case of soccer/football department within the club.



I have been a lifelong player of video games and am very excited about this development within the development towards E-Sports, because I can in fact say that competing in video gaming the highest level is a true sport in the sense of need for uninterrupted concentration over a long period of time. Therefore, fatigue and alertness play an important role just like in other sports.
